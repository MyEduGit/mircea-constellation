person) — a framing taken directly from Paper 196 of *The Urantia Book*

Seventh-day Adventist community from a constructive, friendly, and
experiential perspective.

   uploaded across various Seventh-day Adventist YouTube providers, as

   `messagetostephanos@gmail.com`, for:

2. Attribution of your name and title *Dr. Emanoil Geaboc, Theologian*
   in the channel description and in the video compositions (intro,
   outro, lower-third).
3. A permanent **right to withdraw**: at any point you may ask in
   writing, and we will remove all active editorial references within
   48 hours.

## What we do NOT ask of you, and what we commit NOT to do

- We do not re-upload in full videos you published on other channels.
  JabbokRiver's editorial policy is **catalog & link**, not re-upload.
- We do not use recordings for commercial purposes without your
  explicit, separate agreement.
- We do not associate your name with statements you did not make.
