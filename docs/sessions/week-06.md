## Monday, 2 March 2026

**Theme:** Transactions - Shopping, returning & requesting

### Session 1 (5:15 - 6:30pm)

**Macroskills: Listening, Speaking**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Develop language for everyday transactions: shopping, returning items, making requests | Role-play station cards; Realia (receipts, product packaging, phone); Transaction phrase wall chart | **Units:** VU23519 Element 2 (Cert II) / VU23524 Element 2 (Cert III)<br><br>**Activities:** **Listening:** Listen to transaction dialogues - shopping return, phone complaint, booking appointment. Identify key phrases. **Speaking:** Role-play stations (rotate through 3): Station 1 - buying at a shop; Station 2 - returning a faulty item; Station 3 - asking for help at a counter<br><br>**Language Focus:** Cert II: "I bought this last week and it's broken"; "Can I get a refund?"; Cert III: "I'm calling about my electricity bill"; "I'd like to request an extension" |

### Session 2 (6:45 - 8:30pm)

**Macroskills: Reading, Writing, Digital Skills**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Read transactional documents. Write complaint/request messages. Use digital tools for real-world tasks | Sample complaint letters/emails; Email templates; Computer lab | **Units:** VU23519 / VU23524 + VU23556 / VU23560 / ICTICT214<br><br>**Activities:** **Reading:** Read a sample complaint letter/email; Identify: who is writing, what is the problem, what do they want. **Writing:** Write a short complaint or request (graded by cert level). **Digital Skills:** Cert I - type a short message asking for help; Cert II - compose an email complaint about a faulty item; Cert III - write a formal email requesting a bill extension, attach a document<br><br>**Language Focus:** "Dear Sir/Madam"; "I am writing to..."; "I would appreciate..."; "Please find attached..." |

## Wednesday, 4 March 2026

**Theme:** Managing money & understanding bills

### Session 1 (5:15 - 6:30pm)

**Macroskills: Listening, Speaking**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Understand and discuss bills, payments, and financial services. Settlement focus: managing money in Australia | Sample utility bills (electricity, gas, phone); AMEP Money Topic Pack; Payment method picture cards | **Units:** VU23558 (Cert I) / VU23519 Element 2 (Cert II) / VU23524 Element 2 (Cert III)<br><br>**Activities:** **Listening:** Listen to someone explaining a bill to a friend; Listen to a phone call about a payment plan. **Speaking:** Discuss: "How do you pay your bills?"; Role-play: calling about a bill you don't understand<br><br>**Language Focus:** "The amount due is..."; "The due date is..."; "Can I pay by...?"; "I don't understand this charge" |

### Session 2 (6:45 - 8:30pm)

**Macroskills: Reading, Writing, Digital Skills**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Read and interpret a utility bill. Write notes about bills. Use online bill payment (demo) | Sample utility bills; Bill reading worksheet; Utility company websites (demo mode) | **Units:** VU23558 / VU23519 / VU23524 + VU23556 / VU23560 / ICTICT214<br><br>**Activities:** **Reading:** Read a sample electricity bill - find: account number, amount, due date, usage. **Writing:** Cert I: Copy key information from a bill into a table; Cert II: Write a note to remind yourself of payment details; Cert III: Write a message to a housemate explaining the bill. **Digital Skills:** Cert I - identify numbers and dates on screen; Cert II - navigate to a utility company website; Cert III - demo: online bill payment (simulated), screenshot and save<br><br>**Language Focus:** "Your account number is..."; "Payment is due on..."; "You used [amount] of electricity" |
