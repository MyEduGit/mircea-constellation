## Monday, 2 February 2026

**Theme:** My Learning Plan - Setting up for success

### Session 1 (5:15 - 6:30pm)

**Macroskills: Reading, Writing**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Complete Task B: My Learning Plan. Identify resources for learning support. Explore the "Record of Work" | Assessment Task B: "My Learning Plan" templates; Sample completed learning plans | **Units:** VU22358 (Cert II) / VU22384 (Cert III) / VU23500 (Cert I)<br><br>**Activities:** **Reading:** Read and interpret Task B template questions; Read sample learning plan. **Writing:** Complete Task B: document 3 things about learning plans; list resources (libraries, internet, dictionaries); commit to attendance plan<br><br>**Language Focus:** Modal verbs: "should," "need to," "could"; "I will attend class on..."; "I can use the library to..." |

### Session 2 (6:45 - 8:30pm)

**Macroskills: Listening, Speaking, Digital Skills**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Introduction to Term 1 communication units. Practise opening and closing simple conversations. Digital literacy basics | Role-play Cue Cards ("A New Student" intro); Audio recordings of simple conversations; Computer lab | **Units:** VU23519 (Cert II) / VU23524 (Cert III) + VU23560 / ICTICT214<br><br>**Activities:** **Listening:** Listen to model conversations (audio/teacher demo) - identify openings and closings. **Speaking:** Pair work - practise greetings and introductions; Role-play: meeting someone new. **Digital Skills:** Cert II - set up/check email account, send a test email to teacher; Cert III - create a simple document (name, date, goals)<br><br>**Language Focus:** Openings: "Hi, how are you?"; "Hey! What've you been up to?"; Closings: "Nice talking to you"; "See you next time"; Email: "Dear...," "From..." |

## Wednesday, 4 February 2026

**Theme:** Getting to know your community

### Session 1 (5:15 - 6:30pm)

**Macroskills: Listening, Speaking**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Begin exploring settlement services (Cert I - VU23558). Practise asking for and giving simple personal information | Local area maps; Community service brochures (health, library, transport); Picture cards of community places | **Units:** VU23558 (Cert I) / VU23519 (Cert II) / VU23524 (Cert III)<br><br>**Activities:** **Listening:** Listen to descriptions of local services (doctor, library, Centrelink, school). **Speaking:** Pair work - ask and answer "Where is the...?"; "How do I get to...?"; Describe your neighbourhood<br><br>**Language Focus:** "The doctor is on..."; "You can catch the bus from..."; Prepositions of place: "next to," "near," "on the corner of" |

### Session 2 (6:45 - 8:30pm)

**Macroskills: Reading, Writing, Digital Skills**

| Objectives | Resources | Skills |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Read simple community information texts. Write short sentences about local services. Use the internet to find local services | Local council brochures; Google Maps on devices; Settlement services websites | **Units:** VU23558 (Cert I) / VU23519 (Cert II) + VU23556 / VU23560 / ICTICT214<br><br>**Activities:** **Reading:** Read a simple brochure or flyer about a local service (Cert I: with support; Cert II/III: independently). **Writing:** Write 3-5 sentences about services near home. **Digital Skills:** Cert I - use Google Maps to find a service; Cert II - search for a local service website, bookmark it; Cert III - search, compare two services, write a short summary in a document<br><br>**Language Focus:** "Open from... to..."; "The phone number is..."; "You need to bring..." |
