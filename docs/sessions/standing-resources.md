### Key Knowledge Evidence (KE) Targets

**Cert II (VU23519):**
- Phrasal verbs: "pick up," "looking after"
- Conjunctions: "when," "but," "if"
- Discourse markers: "by the way," "anyway"
- Polite transactional expressions

**Cert III (VU23524):**
- Phrasal verbs: "put up with," "look into"
- Discourse markers: "actually," "I mean," "as I was saying"
- Grammar: Present perfect continuous, past perfect, conditionals (if, unless)
- Compound & complex sentence structures
