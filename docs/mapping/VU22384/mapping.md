## Application / Description

This unit describes the skills and knowledge to identify long and short term goals, review current skills and plan future skills development, develop a learning plan to achieve goals, and develop and maintain a portfolio.

This unit is part of the EAL (English as an Additional Language) Training Package. Predecessor unit: VU21297 - Develop and document a learning plan and portfolio with guidance.
