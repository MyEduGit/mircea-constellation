## Application / Description

This unit describes the performance outcomes, skills and knowledge required by EAL learners to participate in simple conversations and transactions in everyday contexts.

This unit is part of the 22638VIC Certificate I in EAL (Access), which focuses on the development of English language speaking, listening, reading and writing skills directly related to immediate personal and social needs.
